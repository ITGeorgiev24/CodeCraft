<h2 id="about-the-project"> 🔎 About The Project</h2>
<p align="justify">
EduRise is an innovative math learning app designed to make problem-solving faster and more intuitive. With its clean interface and smart features, it helps students understand concepts step by step rather than just memorizing answers. The app offers interactive exercises and personalized learning paths that adapt to each user’s level.

What makes EduRise stand out is its focus on clarity and engagement—complex topics are broken down into simple, easy-to-follow explanations. Whether you're preparing for exams or just improving your skills, EduRise provides a smooth and effective learning experience.

Overall, EduRise is a promising tool for students who want to build confidence in math while learning in a modern, digital way.
</p>
